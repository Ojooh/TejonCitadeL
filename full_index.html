We Are Bitcoin-Only

Tejon Citadel is a bitcoin-only company and we're on a oneway mission to reinforce the citadel around the decentralized bitcoin network. Bitcoin is not about having a lot of money, it's about having a lot of options.

Tejon Citadel is a North American Bitcoin mining operation. Tejon Citadel operates blockchain computing centers that power the global decentralized financial economy. Tejon Citadel is focused on supporting the Bitcoin ecosystem through decentralized proof-of-work mining by providing computing power to the Bitcoin network and processing transactions. We are believers in Bitcoin and our efforts aimed at growing our mining operation demonstrates our commitment to the Bitcoin network.



Decentralized Mining
The more participants involved in Bitcoin mining, the more secure the Bitcoin network. Decentralization is essential to maintain a strong and secure network.
Clean Energy
Increasing operational efficiency by leveraging clean, renewable and scalable energy generation facilities.
Secure Facilities
Access control systems, surveillance, and 24x7 onsite security staff ensure equipment is always secure.
Fortifying the Bitcoin network
Tejon Citadel participates in Bitcoin’s proof of work, improving the network’s decentralization.
Developing Antifragile energy sources
By utilizing colocation Tejon Citadel is able to utilize a majority of non-fossil power sources, keeping mining carbon emissions to a minimum. Additionally Tejon Citadel plans to go even further by tapping into latent energy demand orphaned energy sources.
